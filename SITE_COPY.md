### FAQ

#### Who needs a RoPA?

Almost all organizations processing personal data need a Record of Processing Activities (RoPA). It's a fundamental requirement under GDPR Article 30 and is essential for demonstrating accountability to regulators.

Whether you're a startup or established business, if you handle any personal data of EU residents, you need a RoPA. Our collaborative RoPA system makes this requirement easy to manage.

#### Do I need a DPO?

DPOs are mandatory for public authorities, organizations that conduct large-scale systematic monitoring, or those that process large volumes of special category data (health, biometrics, etc.).

Even if not legally required, a DPO provides valuable oversight and expertise. Our packages cater to both mandatory and voluntary DPO needs.

Not sure if you need one? Book a free consultation and we'll help you determine your requirements.

#### How long does implementation take?

It varies by service and your organization's needs:

- Essential DPO: Up and running within days
- RoPA System: 2-4 weeks for full implementation
- Training Platform: 1-2 weeks to deploy

#### Can I switch between DPO packages?

Absolutely. As your business grows, your compliance needs may change. You can upgrade or downgrade your DPO package at any time.

There's no lock-in. Contact us and we'll help you find the right fit for your current needs, no hassle, no penalties.

#### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

- Bank Transfers
- Credit Cards

Invoicing is done monthly. All pricing is shown excluding VAT. We'll provide all necessary documentation for your records.

#### What's included in the free consultation?

Our free 30-minute consultation is designed to understand your needs and see if we're a good fit:

- Discuss your current compliance situation
