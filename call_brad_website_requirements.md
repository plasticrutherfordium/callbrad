# Call Brad Website Implementation Requirements for Windsurf

## 1. Objective

Implement the revised Call Brad website copy and structure so the website clearly positions Call Brad as a practical GDPR consultancy focused on operational GDPR for lean teams.

The website must make it clear that Call Brad helps startups, SaaS companies, fintechs, SMEs and lean teams build practical privacy operations through:

- a Notion GDPR Teamspace available now;
- DPO support packages;
- GDPR employee training available in Notion and Google Workspace;
- RoPA / connected register setup and maintenance;
- practical GDPR implementation inside the tools teams already use.

The final website should feel clear, practical, direct, commercially sharp and credible. It must not sound like a traditional law firm, generic SaaS platform, policy-template shop, or vague consultancy brochure.

### What is the Notion GDPR Teamspace?
